By using this web application, you (the user) are entrusting the host with your account credentials to Perkiomen Valley School District's eSchoolPlus service.

Within this application, your data is handled as follows:
 - Your account credentials are encrypted when being sent over the internet (provided you connect to this application using HTTPS).
 - Your account credentials are stored in memory only during the login process and are promptly discarded.
 - The temporary token used to fetch your eSchoolPlus classes and assignments is discarded after this process is completed.
 - Your classes and assignments are stored as session data and are discarded when you exit your session.

The host of this web application is not responsible for any misuse of said application by malicious actors.
